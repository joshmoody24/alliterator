Welcome to the Alliterator!

This app is an alliteration generator. It takes two words and finds synonyms of those words that start with the same letter.

  • "Tough Guy" becomes "Brawny Brother"
  • "Funny Picture" becomes "Entertaining Engraving"
  • "Boring Meeting" becomes "Repetitious Rendezvous"
  • "Ice Cream" becomes "Permafrost Paste"
